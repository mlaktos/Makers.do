Three Pillars Of Success

Community

MAKERS’ helps entrepreneurs to turn their vision into reality. Our community of founders, investors and experts is passionate about sharing ideas, experiences and best practices amongst our network of companies

Funding

We’re hands-on and a startup’s first investor. We love to work on product strategy, iterate and fight for customer traction. We invest seed money into ideas or existing teams with a product vision and clear monetization models.

Services

MAKERS provides founders an ideal framework to build, launch and grow companies very quickly. From engineers and designers to office space and network - our goal is to enable tech innovators to focus on what matters most: building sustainable business models.
