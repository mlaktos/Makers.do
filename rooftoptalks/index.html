Why Rooftop Talks?

"Rooftop Talks" is a new, invite-only and free event series produced and hosted by MAKERS. Our second session takes place on August 24th, 2016 at 6:30 pm. Our goal is to connect people within Berlin’s tech scene, exchange knowledge, ideas and experiences.

Get your invite here!

The Speaker

Maks Giordano, Co-Founder and Managing Director at kreait, will hold a thirty-minute keynote about „Corporate Innovation - How to bring companies into the digital age" followed by a Q&A session.

The Timeline

6:30 pm Doors open
7:00 pm Keynote by Maks Giordano
7:30 pm Q&A, hot dogs and networking

The Location

The event takes place on MAKERS‘ rooftop terrace at Invalidenstraße 112, 10115 Berlin.
